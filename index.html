About the show

A theatre-meets-party night built for big choruses, big laughs and big memories.

Hairbrush Hits is a high-energy Brighthearts show packed with nostalgic favourites, feel-good performance and the kind of songs people cannot resist singing along to. It is polished enough for a proper night out, but lively enough to feel like a party from the first number.

Bright colours, bold vocals and instantly recognisable anthems. The show is designed to feel exciting before visitors even arrive at the venue.

Made for audiences
