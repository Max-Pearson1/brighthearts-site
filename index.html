About the show

The ultimate singalong party night out.

THE BRIGHTHEARTS are bringing their ultimate singalong party night out to a venue near you!

There are certain songs that come on the radio and you just want to grab a hairbrush and sing your heart out. Introducing Hairbrush Hits — think classic 80s tunes by artists like Madonna, Bon Jovi and MJ.

The thing is… girls just wanna have fun, don’t they? Gather your tribe. This is going to be a night not to be missed.
